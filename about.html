About Us

The Sound Authority


Founded in Singapore in 2007, The Experts Group aims to bring world-class sound to consumers; to create an “expert” out of every person passionate about music and movies.



The Experts Group, a leading distributor with its headquarters in Singapore, and paid-up capital of S$1,500,000, operates direct subsidiaries in Indonesia, Malaysia and Vietnam. The company manages the resellers, online stores plus 4 direct showrooms with The Experts Sound branding, under its retail division.



The Experts Group is a proud statutory member of the Singapore Business Federation (SBF) – The apex business chamber in Singapore that represents the top 20% of companies in Singapore.



The company began the main business as the exclusive distributor of Bowers & Wilkins, growing the business 6-fold and raising the brand to be voted as Singapore’s Best Entertainment Brand from 2018 to 2022 by the region’s luxury magazine Tatler Singapore. This 15-year relationship ended when Bowers & Wilkins was sold to a new owner Masimo in 2022, who changed its business directions for the brand. In 2024, we were again voted as Singapore’s Best Entertainment Brand with our new partner brand – Monitor Audio, UK’s No.1 home entertainment manufacturer in UK (awarded by Luxlife).



In 2024, we are honoured to be awarded the renowned Dun & Bradstreet Business Eminence Awards (BEA) 2024 for the Silver Category (businesses with revenue of less than S$20 million). This award validates our excellence in corporate governance, sustainability and near-term growth strategies. We are especially honoured that this validation came from Dun & Bradstreet – the leading authority in business creditworthiness.



As of 2024, The Experts Group is the proud regional distributor (in Singapore, Indonesia, Malaysia and Vietnam) for Cambridge Audio, Monitor Audio, Rotel-Michi, Roksan, Triangle, STAX, and SVS. We also distribute/retail other brands individually in each country – DALI, van Den Hul, Control 4, Yamaha and other leading brands.
