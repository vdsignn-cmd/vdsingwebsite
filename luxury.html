The Experts Group Wins Best of Singapore for the 8th Time


Singapore, 4 February 2025 – The Experts Group is proud to announce that it has been awarded the prestigious Best of Singapore (BOS) 2025 by Tatler Singapore, marking our 8th time as Singapore’s top home entertainment and automation solutions provider.



As a trusted lifestyle guide to the best shops and services in Singapore, Best of Singapore 2025 recognises only the finest merchants in their respective fields. This accolade is a testament to The Experts Group’s unwavering dedication to delivering world-class sound and home automation experiences tailored to every customer—from first-time buyers to seasoned audiophiles designing their dream entertainment spaces.



“Winning Best of Singapore for the 8th time is a tremendous honour,” said Mr. Henry Loh, General Director of The Experts Group. “It reinforces our commitment to providing only the best brands, expertise, and customer experiences in the industry. At The Experts Group, we believe that great sound transforms the way people experience music and movies. We are passionate about being a lifelong partner in this journey—helping our customers elevate their listening and viewing experiences, one system at a time.”



Since 2007, The Experts Group has played a pivotal role in shaping Singapore’s premium entertainment landscape. Having first received Best of Singapore in 2018 for its exclusive distribution of Bowers & Wilkins, The Experts Group continues to set industry benchmarks with its latest partnership with Monitor Audio, the UK’s No.1 home entertainment brand (awarded by Luxlife, which celebrates excellence in the luxury industry).



As a leading regional distributor across Singapore, Indonesia, Malaysia, and Vietnam, The Experts Group remains dedicated to curating and delivering the finest in home entertainment, representing globally recognized brands such as Cambridge Audio, Monitor Audio, Rotel-Michi, Roksan, STAX, SVS, van den Hul, and more.
